# Super Mario

Super Mario is a Nintendo character. He likes to jump, has a red hat and always saves the princess. His best friend Luigi has a house where ghosts are living. Both guys like to drive with gokarts. 

## Character traits
* male
* sporty
* funny
* hates bowser
* likes peach

Usually he says:
> It's me, Mario!
